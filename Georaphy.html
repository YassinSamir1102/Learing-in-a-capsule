Geography

Georaphy
Exploration of Body and Space Body geography involves exploring how the body interacts with and occupies physical space, both internally and externally.
Emotional Expression Performers use movement to express emotions, sensations, and narratives, conveying meaning without relying solely on verbal communication.
Spatial Awareness Practitioners develop a heightened awareness of space, experimenting with movement patterns and interactions in different contexts.
Environmental Interaction: Performers may incorporate elements of the environment into their work, interacting with props, architectural features, or natural surroundings.
Cross-disciplinary Practice Body geography draws on techniques from dance, theater, and somatic practices, encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration and experimentation. It is a form of performance art that explores the relationship between the body and geography, encompassing physical, emotional, and environmental dimensions.
